INF351: Information Design Studio I: How to Make a Computer and Why

Daniel Southwick, PhD
Assignments
Using an Arduino kit, I built three computers to serve different purposes.
1) For "Counting Computer," I created a flexible timer with a snooze button reminds users to take breaks.
2) For "Collaborative Computer," I created a computer game that allows two people to test their reaction times against each other.
3) For "Sociable Computer," I used two arduino boards to connect two computers, which allows the users of the two computers to message each other without internet.
