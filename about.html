About Goldie

My Story

I'm a Chinese-American who grew up in the suburbs of the Bay Area, California. A variety of influences and exposures in my childhood led me to be more inclined towards multidisciplinary, human-related studies.

I made the impulsive decision to leave my small suburban bubble and go to college in China, which was quite a dramatic change in perspective, lifestyle, and environment. The experience motivated me to become more career driven and aspirational, but I decided that pursuing computer science wasn't a path that I'd be happy on.

This led me to where I am today: a happy transfer student at the University of Toronto School of Information! I've finally found a study that I really enjoy so I hope to continue my studies in grad school on the impact of information technology on society, politics, and human rights.
