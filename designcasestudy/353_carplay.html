My Story

Carplay Companion App

I decided to leave my bubble and go to university in China, which was quite a dramatic change in perspective, lifestyle, and environment. This experience at the then #1 CS university in the world motivated me to become more career driven and aspirational, but I was unable to return to university in China due to the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic.

This led me to where I am today at the University of Toronto School of Information. I have experience in frontend, backend, UI/UX design, and a bit of circuit design. My tech interests include Autonomous driving, Predictive Analytics, AR/VR, NLP/Conversational Agents, and Data Visualization.
