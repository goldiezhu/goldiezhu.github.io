Design Case Study

GreenP Parking

GreenP Parking

Apple Carplay Dashboard UI for Toronto Parking Mobile App

Overview

Client: Toronto Parking Authority
Platform: iOS - Carplay
Tools: Figma, Mural
Team: Goldie Zhu
Timeline: April 2021

Problem: It is difficult for drivers to find parking in urban cities, like Toronto. It is also unsafe for drivers to use their phones while driving.

Solution: GreenP Carplay App allows drivers to find parking near their destination and safely navigate without distracted driving to their chosen parking location.

Proto-Persona: Dan the Driver

Busy, Working, Frequent Commuter, Tight budget

Goals: Less worries, no more tickets, time efficiency, reliable planning, car is safe.

Frustrations: Fines, hidden fees, late for meetings, parking too far from destination.

"I need to be on time."          "When did I get here?"        "I can't afford another ticket."         "Where did I park?"

Tasks:

  1. Select desired parking location.
  2. Navigate to parking location.
  3. Finalize parking details.

Design Goals

  1. Worry-free process and navigation.
  2. Accessible UX.
  3. Interactions are fast and intuitive.
